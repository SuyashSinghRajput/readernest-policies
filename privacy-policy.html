Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 10, 2025

Welcome to ReaderNest!

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app, ReaderNest ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Account Information
When you create an account on ReaderNest, we collect:

Your email address

Your username

A unique user identifier (UUID)

1.2 User-Generated Content
When you use the App’s social features, we collect the content you choose to share, such as:

Posts and comments

Messages sent to other users

Books you add to your ShareShelf

Profile picture (if you upload one)

1.3 Reading Activity
To enhance your reading experience, we collect:

Reading goals you set

Timer activity (reading session duration)

Streak progress

Participation in reading quests and campaigns

1.4 Location Data
If you choose to enable location-based features (e.g. when sharing book lending offers), we may collect your approximate location. This is always optional and controlled by you.

1.5 Usage Data
We collect anonymized data about how you use the App to improve performance and features, such as:

Screens visited

Features used

App crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

Provide and improve the App’s features

Enable community interaction (posts, comments, messages)

Track your reading progress and goals

Facilitate book sharing and lending

Operate campaigns (e.g. fine waivers)

Ensure the security and integrity of the App

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing
We may share your data:

With trusted service providers (such as our backend provider, Supabase) to operate the App

If required by law or legal process

To protect the rights and safety of our users or the public

Other than that, your data stays private.

4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely in our cloud backend (Supabase servers).
We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices
You can:

Edit or delete your account information within the App

Delete your account — upon which your personal data will be deleted from our servers, subject to any legal retention requirements

Control location permissions via your device settings

6. Children’s Privacy
ReaderNest is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the App or via email.
